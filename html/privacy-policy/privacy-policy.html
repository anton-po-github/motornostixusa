Privacy Policy


Overview
Your right to privacy when using our apps is important to us.

Our privacy policy is simple: We make money by selling services, not by mining your personal information.

We avoid collecting data from you to the extent that our apps and website can provide their functionality without doing so.

When we do collect data, we try to be clear about why we're collecting it and how long we keep it.

We delete your data when we no longer need it and give you the ability to opt out of collection whenever possible.

We use modern security measures to protect any data we collect, and limit access to only those employees who require access to perform their jobs.

We may be legally required to disclose collected data to law enforcement or government agencies in some situations.

Data We Collect
Our iOS and Android apps do not collect any data from you. As a registered user on our web site, we store your email address.

Support Interactions
When handling your support request, we collect your email address and any information you provide voluntarily (such as crash logs or other diagnostic info).

This information is collected solely to help resolve your support inquiry. We retain support emails indefinitely.

Application Usage Analytics
Our apps may collect information about the way you use them. This data is anonymized before it is sent to us, and cannot be used to personally identify you, but you may opt out of usage analytics if you so wish.

We review aggregated usage data for broad patterns, such as how frequently a particular feature is used, or whether users in general prefer one setting over another. This helps us make informed decisions about the future development of our apps.

We do not track individual user behavior in our apps nor do we receive information from your device's displays, cameras, or microphones.

We retain usage analytics data for 30 days.

Crash Logs
If our app crashes while you're using it, anonymized data about the crash will be collected to help us identify the cause of the crash. These "crash logs" contain information such as the state of the app, operating system, and device at the time of the crash, but not your private data.

You may opt out of crash log reporting if you wish.

We retain crash logs for 30 days.

Apple or Google may also collect crash logs if the privacy settings of your device allow it.

Update Checking
Update checking behavior for iOS and Android apps is managed by the operating system, and the relevant information is processed by Apple or Google.

Logging
When you interact with our servers using a web browser or indirectly by network requests sent on your behalf by our apps, some metadata about the request is logged. This metadata may include:

- Your IP address (this may reveal your approximate geographic location)
- The name of the resource requested
- The name and version number of the software making the request (this may reveal information about your web browser, operating system, and their configuration)
- Whether or not the request was successful
- Current date and time

We generally don't look at these logs unless a server is malfunctioning or appears to be the target of malicious activity.

We may look at the information in aggregate to see broad statistics such as how many times our apps have been downloaded, or from which source an unusually high amount of network traffic is arriving.

We retain web server logs for two weeks.

Archived Data
We maintain backups of company data to avoid a catastrophic data loss.

While personal data collected from you expires from our "active" data set according to the schedules mentioned above, it may persist in backups for up to 6 months. These backups are only accessible to specially privileged employees who perform system administration tasks.

We do not restore from backup unless a significant data loss event occurrs.

Questions and Feedback
Our privacy policies may change over time. Our latest privacy information will always be available from this page.

Please contact us should you have any questions about our data collection or privacy policies.
