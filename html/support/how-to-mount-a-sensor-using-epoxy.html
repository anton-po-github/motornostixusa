How to mount a sensor using epoxy

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Use a pen to draw an outline of the sensor on the selected mounting location.

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Prepare the mounting surface by sanding or grinding off the paint within this outline.

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Prepare the epoxy. Evenly spread a thin layer of epoxy on the bottom of the sensor base.

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Attach the sensor base to the surface. Apply gentle but firm pressure to ensure that the sensor base attaches firmly to the surface.

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Use a level to ensure that the sensor base is square to the selected orientation (vertical/horizontal). Ensure that sensor base does not drift from its orientation while the epoxy is curing.

*** NO PICTURE YET ***

Attach the sensor to the base using the bolts provided. Make sure each bolt has a lock washer (provided) to ensure that the bolts don't loosen under vibration conditions. Firmly tighten the sensor bolts to the base.
